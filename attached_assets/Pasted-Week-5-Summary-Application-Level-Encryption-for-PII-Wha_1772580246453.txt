🔐 Encryption Details
Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (Galois/Counter Mode)

Key Size: 256 bits (32 bytes)
IV Size: 128 bits (16 bytes, random per encryption)
Auth Tag: 128 bits (ensures data integrity)
Format: enc:version:iv:authTag:ciphertext

Performance:

Encryption: ~2-3ms per field
Decryption: ~1-2ms per field
Per record (20 fields): 50-100ms total
