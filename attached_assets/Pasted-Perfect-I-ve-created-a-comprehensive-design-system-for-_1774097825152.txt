Full status pipeline tracking (draft → submitted → under review → approved/conditional/rejected)

Document version history (all versions timestamped)

✅ AI Orchestration:

Multi-agent pipeline (not simplified single-agent)
Three agents: Validator → Matcher/Analyzer → Feedback Generator
Batch analysis on submission (not real-time)

✅ Matching & Distribution:

Automatic matching against all funds
Lender distributes manually (no direct fund visibility in system)
