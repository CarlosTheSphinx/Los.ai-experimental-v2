Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? ▼
You get 14 days of full access to any plan you choose. No credit card required. After 14 days, your plan expires unless you add payment information. During your trial, you get full access to all features at that tier level.
Can I change plans anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately. If you upgrade mid-billing cycle, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, the new rate applies to your next billing date.
What happens if I exceed my monthly loan limit? ▼
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You have two options: upgrade to a higher plan immediately, or pay per additional loan at $2-5 per loan depending on your tier. No service interruption.
Do you offer annual discounts? ▼
Yes! Annual plans save you 15% compared to monthly billing. For example, Professional annual is $5,088/year instead of $5,988 if billed monthly. You can switch to annual anytime from your account settings.
What's included in implementation support? ▼
Professional and Enterprise plans include setup assistance from our onboarding team. We'll help you connect your systems, configure your workflows, and train your team. Typical implementation takes 5-10 days.
Can I get a custom quote for my specific needs? ▼
Absolutely. If our standard tiers don't match your needs, our sales team can create a custom Enterprise plan. Contact our sales team and we'll work with you to build the perfect solution.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime? ▼
Month-to-month plans have no contract. You can cancel anytime with one click. Enterprise plans typically have 12-month terms with specific exit clauses. We want to earn your business every month.

Our Story

📷 Founder Photo

From Frustration to Solution

Our founder, James Chen, spent 12 years in mortgage lending. He watched teams struggle with the same problem year after year: processes that couldn't scale without hiring, borrowers left in the dark, systems that didn't talk to each other. When he scaled a portfolio from $5M to $100M per year, he discovered that 70% of his staff was doing repetitive manual work.

In 2022, he partnered with AI researchers to build a solution. Not another loan management tool. Something different. An AI agent that actually automates the work, improves with use, and gives borrowers the transparency they deserve. That's Lendry.AI.

Today, we're trusted by 200+ lending teams processing over $50M in loans. We're proving that lending doesn't have to be stuck in the past. It can be fast, transparent, and actually intelligent.

"To eliminate the friction in lending through intelligent automation. We believe lending teams should focus on relationships, not paperwork. Borrowers should know what's happening, not wonder. That's the future we're building."

— James Chen, Founder & CEO
