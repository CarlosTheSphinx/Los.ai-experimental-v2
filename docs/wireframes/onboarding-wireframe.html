Document Review
Configure how documents are reviewed — automatically by AI, on a schedule, or manually. You can change this per-deal later.
⚡ Default Review Mode
⚡
Automatic
AI reviews documents immediately when uploaded. Fastest turnaround for borrowers.
🕐
Timed / Batch
AI reviews at scheduled times (e.g., every morning at 9 AM). Good for controlled workflows.
✋
Manual
AI only reviews when you click "Auto Process" on a deal. Full control.
📋 Review Checklist
The AI agent checks each document for these criteria:
✓
Document is legible and not corrupted
✓
Document matches the expected category (e.g., bank statement vs. pay stub)
✓
Key data fields are present and readable
✓
Dates are within acceptable range (not expired)
✓
Borrower name matches across documents
